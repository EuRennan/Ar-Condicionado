WiFi LoRa 32

[简体中文]

WiFi LoRa 32 is a classic IoT dev-board designed & produced by HeltecAutomation(TM), it’s a highly integrated product based on ESP32-S3 + SX1262, it has Wi-Fi, BLE, LoRa functions, also Li-Po battery management system, 0.96” OLEDarealso included. It’s the best choice for smart cities, smart farms, smart home, and IoT makers.
